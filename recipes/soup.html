Turnip Soup

We can't all be winners.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups turnips, sliced
  • 1 cup potato, sliced
  • 1 small onion, sliced
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 1 1‚ÅÑ2 tablespoons flour
  • 3 cups chicken stock
  • 1 cup milk

Directions

  1. Place turnips, potatoes, and onions in saucepan.
  2. Cover with small amount of water and simmer until tender; drain.
  3. Mash with potato masher.
  4. Add butter.
  5. Mix flour with 1/4 cup milk.
  6. Add to mixture and blend well.
  7. Add chicken stock.
  8. Mix thoroughly.
  9. Return to heat and bring to a boil.
  10. remove from heat and add remaining 3/4 cup milk.
  11. Reheat to serving temperature taking care not to boil.
  12. Garnish with your choice of parsley, bacon or croutons.
