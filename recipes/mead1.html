Mead Recipe

A taste of blood, from the Northern God himself.

Ingredients

  • Honey
  • Water
  • Yeast
  • Apples

Directions

  1. Cleanse and sterilize all utensils and containers.
  2. Heat water and honey together, until thoroughly mixed.
  3. Slice apples.
  4. Pour water/honey mixture into carboy, add yeast and apples, then cap.
  5. Wait several weeks, then bottle!
